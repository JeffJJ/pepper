Pepper thekken is a wounder variety among black pepper breeds.selected and developed by a farmer of idukki district Mr. TT thomas.the variety produces bunches spikes giving it a grape bunch like appearence. According to scientists of ICAR the yield potential of thekken breed is is 7500 kg/ha, where as other breeds produces a potential yield of 3000 kg/ha.each Spike carry on an average 30-50 sub spikes and 150-400 berriers.the variety is also famous for its luxuriant flowering and disease tolerance.
